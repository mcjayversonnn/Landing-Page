My Childhood Stories

     I was born on October 31, 2005, sa lugar kung saan naroon ang napaka gandang Mayon Volcano, which is sa Bicol. 1 Bicolano ako, pero sarcastically, hindi ako mahilig sa maanghang na pagkain. 2 Sadyang hindi lang ako nasanay kumain nito dahil na rin hindi ako nag-stay sa Bicol noong bata pa ako. 3 My childhood was a colorful variety of games, stories, and endless curiosity. 4 Mahilig akong maglaro noon ng mga larong pambata tulad ng tumbang preso, patintero, pogs, at turumpo. 5 Those games taught me the value of teamwork, strategy, and having fun with friends habang walang iniisip na kahit anong problema. 6 Hindi lang puro laro, mahilig din ako magbasa at manood ng mga kwentong pambata, lalo na yung mga fairy tales at adventure stories na nagpapalawak ng aking imahinasyon. 7 Naalala ko pa yung karoling tuwing magpapasko, gumagawa ng mga instrumento gamit lang yung tantiyan ng mga softdrinks tapos yung golen na nasa loob ng bote para maingay. 8 That scenario was so memorable, nagtuturuan pa kung sino ang unang mag-a-approach sa bahay-bahay. 9 Yung takot at kaba na baka pagalitan kami ng mga matatanda, Pero kapag may isang naglakas-loob, susunod na rin yung iba, tuloy-tuloy na ang pagkanta. 10 Hindi lang basta-basta yung pag-karoling, may mga kompetensya pa kasi hindi lang kayo yung nangangaroling sa lugar namin. 11 Kailangan talaga mag-effort para mapansin at mabigyan ng barya o kaya naman ay mga candies. 12 Minsan, hindi pa tapos kumanta mag sasabi na agad ng “tawad” kase marami ng dumaang bata at nangaroling sa kanila. 13 Isa sa mga pinakamemorable moments ko noong bata ako ay yung paglalaro ko sa labas kasama ng mga childhood friends ko. 14 Kahit mainit o maulan, basta masaya, laro lang nang laro. 15 Mas gusto ko nga lang minsan kapag umuulan is yung maligo ng ulan then sisigaw ng “Lumakas ka pa”, without me knowing na nakaka perwisyo ako sa ibang tao. 16 Hindi lang traditional games ang hilig ko, nahilig din ako maglaro sa computer shop noon. 17 Doon ko unang na-discover ang mundo ng technology, playing games like Counter-Strike, CrossFire, Point Blank, League of Legends and many more. 18 Those hours spent in the computer shop influenced my interest in computers, and little did I know, it would shape my future. 19 Kaya nga ngayon, I’m taking Bachelor of Science in Information Technology, dahil na rin sa hilig ko sa computers noong bata pa ako. 20 These kinds of memories remind me of how simple yet joyful life was when I was still a child. 21

About Teenage Years

     My teenage years were a time of developing love and relationships, self-discovery, and learning new responsibilities. 1 I remember when I was 13, yun yung ka-una-unahang crush and relationship ko. 2 She was my classmate, and nasa iisang circle of friends lang kami. 3 Nagkagusto siya sa akin noon, and eventually, nag-develop din yung feelings ko for her. 4 It was a mix of excitement and nervousness, yung tipong kinikilig ka kapag nagkikita kayo or nag-uusap. 5 We did the usual things people do in a relationship, like dating and updating each other about our day. 6 Pero syempre, being young and inexperienced, there were also moments of misunderstandings, arguments, tsaka tampuhan. 7 Still, those moments taught me a lot about communication, trust, and how to handle emotions. 8 Nung teenager din ako, nagkaroon na ako ng personal cellphone, which was a big deal for me noon. 9 Natuto ako gumamit ng cellphones, from texting to calling, and even exploring basic apps. 10 Pero kahit may cellphone na ako, hindi nawala sa akin yung paglalaro sa computer shop. 11 Ang ginagawa ko, kapag na-lowbat yung cellphone ko, tsaka ako maglalaro sa computer shop. 12 Doon ko nakikita yung mga kaibigan ko, and we’d spend hours playing games like what I said on my childhood stories. 13 It was a balance between staying connected through my phone and enjoying the thrill of gaming with friends. 14 Those moments were unforgettable. 15 Teenage years were also a time of learning house chores and other essential life skills. 16 Natuto akong maglinis ng bahay, maglaba, mag-saing, at magluto ng simple meals or yung mga priniprito lang like hotdog and eggs. 17 My parents made sure na hindi lang ako focused sa academics since I was high school that time, but also prepared sa tunay na buhay. 18 I remember my mom teaching me how to cook adobo, kase ayun yung pinaka madaling lutuin among other dishes. 19 and my dad naman po is an OFW so we’re making memories through video call. 20 Those lessons were helpful experience to me, since they made me more independent and responsible. 21 At the same time, I was also learning how to manage my time, balancing schoolwork, relationships, and personal hobbies. 22

Throughout College Experiences

     About where I am now, It’s more serious compared to the past. 1 College defines what we are in the future kumbaga, ito yung stepping stone natin para makarating sa ating finish line. 2 My first year in college wasn’t exactly the happiest, especially during the first day or first week. 3 It felt like I was back to zero again kasi nga mga bagong kaklase yung ma-meet ko. 4 Kailangan ko pang mag-adjust at makipag-socialize para maging komportable sa mga bago mong kasama o magiging kaibigan. 5 I chose the course Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. 6 which as I mentioned in my childhood and teenage years, aligned with my love for computers. 7 Pero sa totoo lang, wala akong masyadong knowledge about programming languages and other software. 8 Kaya naman naging mahirap sa akin yung first year. 9 There were times na parang gusto ko nang mag shift ng course, pero sa tulong ng mga professors at friends, parang mas naging magaan siya. 10 Doon ko na-realize na, “Ah, ganito pala yon. 11 kaya ko naman pala,” and slowly, things started to make sense. 12 By the second year, medyo nakapag-adjust na ako, and tuloy-tuloy na lang yung ginagawa ko. 13 I focused on doing school works, reviewing for quizzes, and preparing for exams. 14 Masasabi ko rin na may knowledge na ako sa mga programming languages tulad ng Python, Java, C#, and yung MySQL for databases. 15 I’m a grade-conscious person, so hindi okay sa akin yung pasado lang. 16 Gusto ko lagpas sa passing score. 17 I always try to do my best, kahit na minsan sobrang exhausting na. 18 There were sleepless nights spent coding, debugging, and studying, pero fulfilling naman kapag nakikita mo yung results ng hard work mo. 19 Hindi lang academics yung focus ko, natuto rin akong mag-balance ng time for hobbies and relaxation, like playing online games or watching series kapag may free time. 20 I can say that I’ve grown not just academically, but also personally. 21
