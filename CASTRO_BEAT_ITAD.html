The Musics That Define Me

R&B

The Weeknd - After Hours

The Weeknd is a Canadian singer known for his unique blend of R&B and pop. "After Hours" is a hauntingly beautiful track from his critically acclaimed album of the same name.

Bruno Major - Easily

Bruno Major is a British singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics. "Easily" is a soothing track that explores themes of love and vulnerability.

Frank Ocean - Ivy

Frank Ocean is an American singer-songwriter celebrated for his introspective and experimental music. "Ivy" is a nostalgic track that reflects on love and heartbreak.

Daniel Caesar - Hold Me Down

Daniel Caesar is a Canadian singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and emotional lyrics. "Hold Me Down" is a powerful track that explores themes of love and devotion.

Hip-Hop

Kendrick Lamar - HUMBLE.

Kendrick Lamar is an American rapper and songwriter known for his thought-provoking lyrics and powerful performances. "HUMBLE." is a hard-hitting track that showcases his lyrical prowess.

21 Savage - Prove It

21 Savage is an American rapper known for his gritty lyrics and distinctive voice. "Prove It" is a powerful track that explores themes of loyalty and trust.

Future, Metro Boomin - Type Shit

Future and Metro Boomin are a dynamic duo known for their innovative beats and catchy hooks. "Type Shit" is a high-energy track that showcases their unique style.

Kendrick Lamar - Money Trees

"Money Trees" is a standout track from Kendrick Lamar's critically acclaimed album "good kid, m.A.A.d city." The song explores themes of ambition and the pursuit of success.

Pop

Taylor Swift - Midnight Rain

Taylor Swift is an American singer-songwriter known for her narrative songwriting and versatile music style. "Midnight Rain" is a captivating track that explores themes of love and reflection.

Drake - Teenage Fever

Drake is a Canadian rapper and singer known for his catchy hooks and introspective lyrics. "Teenage Fever" is a nostalgic track that reflects on past relationships and youthful experiences.

Cup of Joe is a Filipino band known for their heartfelt lyrics and catchy melodies. "Multo" is a beautiful track that explores themes of love and longing.

Amiel Sol - Sa Bawat Sandali

Amiel Sol is a Filipino singer-songwriter known for his soulful voice and heartfelt lyrics. "Sa Bawat Sandali" is a touching track that explores themes of love and devotion.
