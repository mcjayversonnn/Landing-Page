Boracay

Why This Place is Special to Me

Boracay is known for its powdery white sand beaches and vibrant nightlife. It is a place where I can relax, enjoy water activities, and witness stunning sunsets.

How to Get There

Boracay is accessible via Caticlan or Kalibo Airport. From the airport, you can take a van or bus to the Caticlan Jetty Port, followed by a short ferry ride to the island.

Other Places to Visit in the Area

While in Boracay, visit Puka Shell Beach, Mount Luho, and Bulabog Beach. These spots offer unique experiences beyond the famous White Beach.
