Where I Long to Be

International

Mount Fuji, Japan

Mount Fuji, Japan's iconic mountain. I've always wanted to visit Mount Fuji for its iconic, almost surreal beauty. For me, there's something so peaceful yet powerful about seeing it up close.

Seoul, South Korea

Seoul is a dream mix of buzzing city life and pockets of tradition. I'd go for their food (Street-market tteokbokki and Endless BBQ). I also want to visit historical landmarks like Gyeongbokgung Palace.

Matterhorn, Switzerland

The Matterhorn looks just like a mountain from a postcard tall, pointy, and unbelievably beautiful. I want to admire the stunning Matterhorn peak and enjoy the serene beauty of the Swiss Alps.

Local

El Nido, Palawan

Turquoise water, secret lagoons, and those dramatic limestone cliffs. El Nido looks like paradise. I would like to spend days island-hopping, snorkeling, and eating fresh seafood by the beach.

Boracay

White sand so fine it feels like powder, insane sunsets, and that lively beachfront vibe. Boracay seems like the perfect mix of relaxation and fun. I'd love to walk on their white sand beaches and experience the ambience.

Batanes

Rolling hills, stone houses, and rugged coastlines make Batanes feel like a hidden gem. I'd rent a bike to explore the endless green landscapes and maybe meet some of the locals, known for their warmth and stories.
